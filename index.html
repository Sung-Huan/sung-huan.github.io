Computational System BioMedicine Lab

We are a group of people who are interested in Bioinformatics. We are focusing on NGS and proteomics data analysis and applying to various biomedical studies. Our current directions are multi-omics integration, deep learning application, genome annotation, and single cell RNA-Seq and proteomics analysis.
